About Daawi

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: Feeling each other's pain and giving as we would like to be given — like trees in a forest going through tough times, sharing food through their roots so no one stands alone.

Vision: A world where empathy leads action — where humans across the globe support one another in times of need.

We provide direct support to struggling individuals and communities across the planet by offering food, medical assistance, and raising awareness to inspire others to give. Our work is rooted in compassion and the belief that when we share, we all grow stronger.

  • Food Support
  • Medical Assistance
  • Community Building
  • Awareness Campaigns

Upcoming & Past Events

Join us at our upcoming events or view the impact of our past initiatives.

Dinner for Sudan

May 6, 2025

The Hague

A special dinner event to raise funds for humanitarian relief in Sudan. We shared stories of resilience, enjoyed a communal meal, and gathered resources for those in need.

Impact

Thanks to your generous donations, we raised €1,904 for humanitarian relief in Sudan. Your support has made a significant impact on the lives of those in need.

Gaza Fundraiser x Conscious Kitchen

March 27, 2025

Conscious Kitchen, The Hague

Our successful collaboration with Conscious Kitchen raised essential funds for humanitarian aid in Gaza. Participants enjoyed conscious dining while supporting a vital cause.

Impact

Thanks to your generosity and our collaboration with Conscious Kitchen, we successfully raised €1,006 for Gaza. Due to the ongoing closure of all crossings and the restriction of humanitarian aid, we are currently unable to deliver food and supplies. The funds are being securely held and will be used as soon as access is possible. We will update you on the impact of this event as soon as the situation allows.

Free Palestine x Bonobo

February 27, 2025

Bonobo, The Hague

A collaborative event with Bonobo where we gathered in solidarity to raise awareness and funds for humanitarian efforts in Palestine. The community came together for this important cause.

Impact

Thanks to your generous donations, we raised €954 and were able to provide food packages for 25 families in Gaza during Ramadan. Through your support, our shared meal extended beyond borders—until we can sit together in person, we've found a way to eat together from afar. We feel each other's pain and give as we would like to be given.
